Empathy: Forget Me Nots

Forget-me-nots are often given as gifts to show support and encouragement. Giving someone a bouquet of these flowers is a way to show that you care and that you are there for them. Thus, it is associated with empathy.

Gratitude: Sunflower

Sunflowers are known for their bright, warm, cheerful appearance and are often given as gifts to express thanks and appreciation for someone's unwavering support.

Kindness: Daffodils

In the language of flowers, daffodils convey the message of hope, happiness, and kindness. They are often given to someone who is going through a difficult time or as a gesture of support and encouragement.

Respect: Pink Carnation

Pink carnations are a popular flower that represent respect and admiration. They are also associated with the idea of a mother's love, making them ideal bouquet choice for showing respect.

Wisdom: Lavender

Lavender is believed to have a range of medicinal properties and is often used in aromatherapy to promote mental clarity and improve cognitive function, making it the perfect flower to represent wisdom.
